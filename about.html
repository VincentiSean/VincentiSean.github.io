G2H2 Pittsburgh is celebrating 11 years!

Gay Guy Happy Hour takes place the second Friday of every month at a different location in Pittsburgh. Hosted by JR, Wayne, Michael, and Daniel, it's a great way to meet new people and relax with friends in a different atmosphere every month!

G2H2 brings the party to local businesses that open their doors to the LGBT community!
